Our Story

"I was only 8 years old when I discovered how passionate I was about dancing. Dance filled me with such happiness especially, when I danced to the tunes of bollywood songs and eventually ended up in specializing in it. We started Roots entertainment with an intention to to give all the dance lovers a platform to explore their passion. It is a gregarious place where anyone can learn how to dance"

- Sneha Salve

"I have danced to live and lived to dance ever since I have known that there is something as dancing specially Zumba.
